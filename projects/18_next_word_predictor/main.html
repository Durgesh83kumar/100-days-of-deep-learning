MS Dhoni – Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

Who is MS Dhoni?

MS Dhoni is a former Indian cricketer and one of the most successful captains in cricket history.

What is MS Dhoni’s full name?

His full name is Mahendra Singh Dhoni.

When and where was MS Dhoni born?

He was born on 7 July 1981 in Ranchi, Jharkhand, India.

Why is MS Dhoni called “Captain Cool”?

Because of his calm behavior in high-pressure situations.

Early Life and Background

What was MS Dhoni’s early life like?

He played football and badminton and worked as a Ticket Collector before cricket.

Which state did MS Dhoni represent in domestic cricket?

He represented Bihar and later Jharkhand.

What is MS Dhoni’s jersey number?

His jersey number is 7.

International Career

When did MS Dhoni make his international debut?

He made his ODI debut in 2004 against Bangladesh.

What was MS Dhoni’s breakthrough performance?

His 148-run innings against Pakistan in 2005.

What is MS Dhoni’s highest ODI score?

His highest ODI score is 183*.

Captaincy and Achievements

When did MS Dhoni become India’s captain?

He became captain in 2007.

Which ICC trophies did India win under his captaincy?

2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy.

IPL Career

Which IPL team does MS Dhoni lead?

He is the captain of Chennai Super Kings (CSK).

Retirement and Legacy

When did MS Dhoni retire from international cricket?

He retired on 15 August 2020.

Why is MS Dhoni still popular?

Because of his leadership, humility, and achievements.
